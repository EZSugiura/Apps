Last updated: June 1, 2025

Summary: Energy Calc Notebook does not collect, store, or share any personal information you enter. All calculations are performed locally on your device. We display ads through Google AdMob, which may collect certain advertising-related data as described below.

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how EZ Company ("we," "us," or "our") handles information in connection with the mobile application Energy Calc Notebook (the "App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

All numerical values you enter into the App for energy calculations are processed entirely on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any of this data on external servers.

2.2 Automatically Collected Information (via Google AdMob)

The App displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. Google may automatically collect the following information to serve relevant ads:

  • Device identifiers (e.g., Advertising ID)
  • IP address and approximate location
  • Device type, operating system version, and language settings
  • App interaction data (e.g., ad impressions, clicks)
  • Diagnostic data

This data is collected and processed by Google LLC in accordance with their own privacy policy. We do not have direct access to or control over this data.

3. How We Use Information

We do not use or process any personal information directly. The advertising data collected by Google AdMob is used by Google for:

  • Delivering and personalizing advertisements
  • Measuring ad performance
  • Fraud prevention

4. Data Storage

The App stores your calculation settings and preferences locally on your device using the operating system's standard local storage (SharedPreferences). This data never leaves your device and is deleted when you uninstall the App.

5. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party service:

  • Google AdMob — advertising platform operated by Google LLC.
    Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
    Opt-out: You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings (Android: Settings → Google → Ads; iOS: Settings → Privacy → Tracking).

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the App, please contact us and we will take steps to delete such information.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your personal data, including:

  • The right to access information about data collected about you
  • The right to request deletion of your data
  • The right to opt out of personalized advertising

Since we do not collect personal data directly, most requests should be directed to Google LLC for AdMob-related data.

8. Data Security

All calculation data remains on your device and is never transmitted. We take reasonable measures to protect the App from security vulnerabilities, though no software is completely immune to risk.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of the App after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
